Our approach

Real care. Real change.

Crossroads Holistic Clinic combines evidence-informed medicine with attentive, personalised support. Your doctor takes time to understand symptoms, lifestyle, history, goals, and what has or has not worked before.

Care is delivered through secure telehealth consults, clear treatment planning, ongoing review, and practical next steps. We focus on sustainable progress rather than rushed appointments or one-size-fits-all answers.

Questions

Common things people ask before booking.

Is this clinic available Australia-wide?

Crossroads is designed around telehealth, so many consultations can be completed online. Some services may depend on clinical suitability, location, and regulatory requirements.

Will I be prescribed treatment at my first appointment?

Only if it is clinically appropriate. Your doctor will review your history, current health, goals, and any relevant risks before discussing treatment options.

Do you replace my regular GP?

No. Crossroads can support specific holistic care needs, but we encourage patients to maintain a relationship with their regular GP for general and emergency health matters.
