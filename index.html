    Compliance-aware

    Important guardrails.

    This site can be product-aware without selling prescription medicines directly. Public product discovery needs careful wording and clinical disclaimers.

    Can patients buy cannabis products before speaking to a doctor?

    No. The product library is for education and preference capture. Any prescription product requires a valid clinical assessment and lawful pharmacy dispensing.

    Can the TGA product list be imported?

    Yes. The interface is ready for a bulk import. The TGA describes these as unapproved medicinal cannabis products supplied through SAS/AP pathways, so the site should avoid calling them approved consumer products.

    Can Canna Reviews data be made public here?

    Yes, if you provide data you are allowed to use. Protected MedAuth content should not be scraped, but an authorised export can be imported into a searchable public library.
