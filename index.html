Questions

Before you book.

Is Crossroads available Australia-wide?

Many appointments can be completed by telehealth. Service availability can depend on clinical suitability, location, and regulatory requirements.

Will treatment be prescribed straight away?

Only if it is clinically appropriate. Your clinician will review your history, current health, goals, and risks before discussing any treatment pathway.

Do I still need my usual GP?

Yes. Crossroads can support specific holistic care goals, but it does not replace emergency care or your regular GP relationship.
